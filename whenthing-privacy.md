### WhenThing Privacy Policy

We don’t keep, track, store, or pass on any of your information. 

#### The only exception to this:

We use Doorbell.io to collect feedback. If you choose to send us feedback, your data will be collected according to their privacy policy, available here: https://doorbell.io/privacy-policy.
In this instance, we'll have the details you sent us, but we won't send you email unless you explicitly request a reply.
