# CareShares Privacy Policy
Last updated: April 2026
  
### CareShares does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

All information you enter — care schedules, journal entries, and notes — is stored exclusively in your personal iCloud account using Apple's iCloud Drive and Calendar services. This data is governed by Apple's iCloud Terms of Service and is never transmitted to any server operated by the developer.

CareShares does not collect, use, or share any personal data. The App Store and where applicable TestFlight may collect crash reports and usage statistics on Apple's behalf; this is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Local notifications, if enabled, are scheduled on-device and no notification content leaves your device.
